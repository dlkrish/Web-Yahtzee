Welcome to Yahtzee Lite!


You have 3 rolls to get a large straight (1, 2, 3, 4, 5 or 2, 3, 4, 5, 6).

After a roll, you can choose to keep the number you have for a die by clicking the "keep" button.

If you get a large straight after 3 rolls, you win!

To start, click "Play" and then "Roll" to start your first roll.




Click play to start!




Click a keep button to keep the value of a die for the next roll(s).
